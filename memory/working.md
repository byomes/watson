User: What does the sermon say about faith?
Watson: Faith can break out at any time and when it does, it is absolutely life-changing. This is illustrated in the story of the two criminals on Jesus' cross. One criminal insults Jesus, while the other believes and asks to be remembered by Jesus. The believing criminal's faith leads him to heaven, while the unbelieving criminal goes to hell.
