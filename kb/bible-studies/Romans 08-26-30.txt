Observation W verse Additional notes
in the same way what Rom 8:26 the way that the spirit gives believers hope, we also have help from the Spirit, both are a part of the Christian life
Spirit who Rom 8:26 the third member of the Trinity, the Holy Spirit of God
helps us what Rom 8:26 the HS not only is the promise of our future, but our helper and guide today and tomorrow while we are waiting
weakness what Rom 8:26 the "weakness" here is likely a furhter reference to the war between flesh and spirit
do not know what to pray what Rom 8:26 there are times when believers are either ignornant of or confused by the spiritual war that faces them
Spirit Himself prays what Rom 8:26 this does not refer to speaking in tongues. First, the Spirit alone is in view, not the Spirit in conjuntion with the believer
second, the "us" is the collective group of all Christians. Speaking in tongues is a gift, but it is not given to all Christians.
intercedes what Rom 8:26 intercession is the activity of prayer for another. Here, the Spirit prays for us the believers
wordless groans what Rom 8:26 another reason this can not be speaking in tongues, the prayer of the Spirit is said to be "wordless" thus not a language
searches our hearts what Rom 8:27 the Spirit searches our hearts as He prays for us, this means there is no "hiding" from the Spirit or His prayer
the will of God what Rom 8:27 the Spirit is not praying for us alone, but rather for the will of God to be done in and through us
all things work together what Rom 8:28 here the sovereign overarching will of God is seen as working out in "all things" both good and bad from our perspective
love Him & called by Him who Rom 8:28 often when cited, this verse is missing the latter part of the text. The "good" (which is God's will) is worked out for those who
BOTH love Him and are called according to His purpose. Loving God in one's own actions is not the sole standard
foreknew what Rom 8:29 the foreknowledge of God in this text also likely refers to His "fore-causing" of a relationship to occur
predestined what Rom 8:29 the predestined of God is the promise of the future end of eternity with Him, thus those in relationship with Him are
guaranteed a future with Him
to be conformed to the image what Rom 8:29 the third part of the activity of God in view here is that He works in us to cause us to conform to be like Christ
of Christ what Rom 8:29 there is no one else in which we are able to conformed by God than Christ alone
the firstborn of many what Rom 8:29 Jesus is the firstborn of the resurrrection. He is the first human to be resurrected and receive a glorified body
He also called what Rom 8:30 being "called" in this sense is likely the calling to the family began by the ressurected Christ
he also justified what Rom 8:30 the concept of justification is the "guilty, not guilty" declaration of God. Though we are guilty of our sin, the penalty is paid
and we are considered free from the punishment our sin would have been due
He also glorified what Rom 8:30 the state of glorification is the final state we all look forward to in eternity. We will be given new bodies that will never
age or decay, be free from sin, and live in an eternal state of grace with God Himself.
