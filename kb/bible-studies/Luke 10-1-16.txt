two by two | what | Luke 10:1 | this provided support and security, but it also meets the Old Testament's need for valid testimony (Num 35:30, Deut 19:15)

 |  |  | A harvest also gives a sense of urgency in that crops need to be harvested when they are ripe so they aren't lost or spoiled
workers are few | what | Luke 10:2 | it's not that workers are not present to do the task, but that they pale in comparison to the size of the task
Lord of the harvest | who | Luke 10:2 | this illustrates God's providence not only the workers, the field, but of the harvest itself.
