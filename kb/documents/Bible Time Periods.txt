The story of the Bible takes place throughout human history. The Word of God is comprised of 66 books,
written through more than 40 human authors, over a period of more than 1500 years. To better understand
the historical settings, it is helpful to recognize the time periods that make up The Bible Timeline. Each period
is listed below with a basic theme and the sections of Scripture that take place in that period.
