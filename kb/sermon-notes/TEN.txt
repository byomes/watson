causes a loss to someone else's property, provision, or family.

1: actual theft
2: kidnapping
3: dishonest gain
4: accidental loss

Paying restitution for items you were securing for someone else
(22:7)
Paying restitution for injury to a borrowed animal (22:14)

Paying restitution for injury to a borrowed animal (22:14)
